Austin Council on Adoptable Children

The Austin Council on Adoptable Children is a support group for adoptive families.

Meeting Time:

We meet on the second Monday of every month from 6:30-8:30.

Bring the Kids!

We offer free childcare as well as a Teen Group, led by a licensed professional counselor.

Meeting Place:

We meet at 14000 Summit Drive (Department of Family and Protective Services Office)

Our Purpose

Through the years the goals and purposes of the Austin Council on Adoptable Children (COAC) have changed to meet the needs of its membership. We have been there for families in crisis, we have been there to celebrate victories, we have been there to offer shoulders to cry on, and we are here for you. Come join with us as we work together to build strong families. You are always welcome to share.

What We Offer

  • Email reminders and updates
  • Quality speakers
  • Support to attend any adoption related conference
  • Network of other families to consult.
  • Free childcare and a support group for teens
